Starfield

Move the mouse right to increase or left to decrease speed

Starfield is an implementation of Coding Challenge #1 from The Coding Train by Daniel Shiffman.

A field of stars flies toward the viewer, simulating warp speed travel through space. Each star is positioned in 3D space and projected onto the 2D canvas, growing larger as it approaches. Moving the mouse left slows the warp, moving right accelerates it.
